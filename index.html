About Me

With over 10 years of professional experience, I specialise in transforming quality assurance from a bottleneck into a strategic advantage. My goal is to make complex testing concepts accessible and actionable for teams of all sizes. I have successfully led QA teams and managed quality efforts for many software products, ensuring delivery excellence.


My expertise lies in architecting, scaling, and maintaining robust automated test frameworks across a wide range of platforms and technology stacks. I am experienced in strategic test and release planning, ensuring that quality is built in from the start.


Beyond that, I am committed to driving the Quality Assurance industry forward. I am a chapter leader in BrowserStack's Software Testing Meetup Group hosted in Dublin.
